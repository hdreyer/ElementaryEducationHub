Special Education Resources

Below are links to a selection of great resources to help parents and teachers with special education students

  • Actively Learn
    Online curriculum with scaffolding
  • Do2Learn
    Free activities for behavioral regulation and social skills
  • EmotionalABCs
    Social-emotional learning resources for early elementary grades
  • Equal Access to Distance Curriculum
    Distance Learning curriculum resources
  • IXL.com
    Free curriculum with diagnostics
  • UFT's Students With Disabilities Resources
    Online materials for building literacy
  • Skill Champ
    Foundational skills practice tool that supports specific learning needs
  • Seeing AI
    Free app for visually impaired students - uses AI to communicate the world to students
  • Google Chrome Extensions for Students with Special Needs
    Help with text-to-speech, focus, comprehension, and readability
  • Headsprout
    Reading resouces - free for rest of school year
  • Intervention Central
    Clearinghouse for free RTI materials
  • Newsela
    Leveled reading resources - free for rest of school year
  • New York Public Library Free Tutoring
    Offers live tutoring and homework help
  • Intervention Central
    RTI resources including behavioral interventions
  • BookShare
    For students that have barriers to reading - free access for remote learning
  • ModMath
    Blank virtual grid sheets to assist students with mathmatical notation
